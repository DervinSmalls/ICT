Mission Statement

Inland Classical Theatre invites classical works into new contexts to examine and interrogate our contemporary world. We inspire and challenge audiences with the production of accessible, well-crafted theatre and forward the development of all theatre artists through training and education.


Abby Burlingame

Artistic Director

Jeffrey St. George

Executive Director

Jeffrey St. George as Executive Director blends his long time love of theater with his life long love for business administration. As a child Jeff enjoyed drafting work order requests for his parents to labor through. Transitioning into adulthood Jeff has spent time on stage performing as Hamlet, The Nurse, Bat Boy, Macbeth and many more. He has produced with Oregon Adventure Theater and Spokane Shakespeare Society and his love for the art spans from onstage to backstage. His experience in business management is focused on the service of underserved community populations. He looks forward to blending his love of the arts, with his passion for service to his community.
