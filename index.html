INLAND CLASSICAL THEATRE

Theatre on the wing



Classical works for a contemporary world.

Inland Classical Theatre is a new theatrical venture based in Spokane, WA. We produce everything from Greek theatre to Shakespeare to modern classics, with plenty more inbetween. We believe in looking back to look forward, examining and sharpening our theatrical roots in order to blaze ahead into our shared future. Will you join us?.

The Board

Our executive team and board is comprised of local theatre artists with expertise in and a passion for producing classical work.

This Season

Join us this summer for two readings in repertory of Shakespeare’s The Taming of the Shrew and John Fletcher’s sequel A Tamer Tamed. Our full inaugural production will launch in Fall 2025.
