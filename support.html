INLAND CLASSICAL
THEATRE





Inland Classical Theatre invites classical works into new contexts to examine and interrogate our contemporary world. We inspire and challenge audiences with the production of accessible, well-crafted theatre and forward the development of all theatre artists through training and education.



As we set foot into this brave new world every cent matters. Our promise to you is any every contribution you make will be cherished and used responsibly to bring new opportunity to the greater Spokane area.
